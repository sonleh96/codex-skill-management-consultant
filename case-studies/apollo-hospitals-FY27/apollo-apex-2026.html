Governing Thought · SCQA Resolution

Apollo cannot simultaneously maximise bed expansion, digital burn, and shareholder returns — the market is paying for execution certainty, not optionality. The winning position is Growth + Returns: use the HealthCo demerger to harvest digital optionality without burning cash, cap 24|7 at ₹450 Cr with binding 2-of-3 gates, and allocate 25–30% of residual FCF to shareholders. Projected TSR CAGR: 17% vs 8–10% for all alternatives.
